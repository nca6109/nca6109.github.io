How to Play

Each turn, pick an option of either "attack", "reload", or "dodge" (when each is available).

  • Attack: Use one of the bullets in your chamber to dodge a reloading opponent
  • Reload: Take your turn to load another bullet into the chamber of your gun
  • Dodge: Avoid an incoming shot from the opponent

Alternatively, you will collect cards are you defeat opponents. Instead of using a basic option, you could use a card on your turn instead. Hover over a card to get a description of what it does.

After you click "End Turn" you and your opponent's move will play out and you either go on to the next round or one of you will die. After an opponent is defeated, you move on to fight the next opponent until you defeat Golden Gun Gary.
